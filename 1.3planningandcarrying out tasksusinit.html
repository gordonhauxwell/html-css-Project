1.3 - Planing and carrying out tasks using IT

This is the easy part. All you need is a Trello board setting up using the Trello website. Its easy to do and even easier to follow once set up. The best bit of all its even free to use so wont cost you or the company any charges or monthly / yearly subscription charges You can set up multiple columns like to do list, a doing list and then finally a done list. Also for each item on the list you can set up and personalise an individual member or members of that team or even the whole team and if needed multiple teams which tells everyone at once who should be doing what with who and when.

  • To do list which is to tell who or what team what tasks they have to do.
  • A column that shows what people are working and who should be working on it if its more than one person or team.
  • A done list which will tell you who or what team has completed their tasks.
  • You can also check on when the activity was completed or moved to doing column.
